    Exhibition Gallery

    RWA (Royal West of England Academy) hosts an annual exhibition and displays a variety of artworks including drawing, painting, photography, printmaking, sculpture, illustration and mixed media submissions and is a showcase of some of the most exciting artists from across the country and beyond. For this exhibition I was successful in submitting one of my wall based sculptures, my silicon, silicon gun.
