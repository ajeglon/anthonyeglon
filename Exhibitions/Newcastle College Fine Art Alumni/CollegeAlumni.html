    • Newcastle College Alumni Exhibition (June 2019)

    Exhibition Gallery

    An exhibition of artwork by alumni of the Fine Art department at Newcastle College. For this exhibition I decided to focus more on display wall based pieces rather than the floor sculptures I had been developing, this allowed me to approach this exhibition in a new direction as not since the Open Studios had I not displayed a floor sculpture as my centre piece. I decided to exhibit my phallic wall pieces and experiment with placements of the sculptures causing me to further play on the suggestive themes by placing one at head level and the other at crotch level.
