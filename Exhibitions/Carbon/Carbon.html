    • Carbon (March 2016)

    Carbon was part of an experimental exhibition series named “TESTBED 16” produced by second year Fine Art students of Northumbria University. The exhibition “Carbon” was developed through several discussions about diverse ideas of Nature and as such each artwork presented spoke to some degree about an aspect of nature. Due to a nature themed exhibition I presented a sculpture I created whilst exploring and experimenting with textures which resembled a coral reef. Despite it’s appearance on closer look the “coral” is completely made up of fingers, presenting a combination of nature and man made. Unfortunately there are only limited photos from this exhibition and none include this piece so I have attached a general photo of the sculpture that was included.
